2.IEEE Micro-chip Summer Camp

What did I do:
I joined the IEEE Micro-chip Summer Camp in 2017. I did a research on building up a complex noise maker using a micro-chip. What did I achieve:
Knowledge about micro-chip and skills to create, design, and build a electronic circuit.
